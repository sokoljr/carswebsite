Mercedes-Benz centr

Anytime and anywhere.
My Mercedes-Benz world.

We get you where you want to go – with our flexible mobility service car2go, which enables you to hire a car of the smart brand on the spur of the moment and to leave it anywhere you wish within the relevant operating area at the end of your journey. You can find the vehicles via the smartphone app or the website – you pay only for the time you actually hire the car – billing is by the minute. Parking charges, fuel costs, taxes and insurance are already included. Kind on your wallet, and kind on the environment, too – the smart fortwo is a particularly environment-friendly car. car2go additionally deploys electric vehicles in selected cities. In this way, the pioneer of flexible mobility concepts offers you an opportunity to experience electric mobility for yourself.
